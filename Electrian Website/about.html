Our Story

Founded in 2010, SA Electric has been serving the San Antonio community with excellence and dedication. We started with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional electrical services that our customers can trust.

Over the years, we've grown from a small team of dedicated electricians to a full-service electrical company, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains unchanged.

Our Mission

We strive to be the most trusted electrical service provider in San Antonio by:

  • Delivering exceptional service quality
  • Maintaining the highest safety standards
  • Providing honest and transparent pricing
  • Ensuring customer satisfaction
